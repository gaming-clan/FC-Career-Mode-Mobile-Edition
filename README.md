## üéÆ Overview

FC Career Mode Mobile Edition is a single-player football simulation game designed to fill the gap left by EA FC Mobile's lack of deep career mode experiences. Inspired by EA FC 26's beloved career modes, this project aims to deliver authentic football management experiences optimized for mobile devices.

Whether you want to build a dynasty as a manager, experience the life of a professional footballer, oversee club operations as a sporting director, or create your own club from scratch, this game offers multiple paths to football glory.

### Key Highlights

- **Four Distinct Game Modes** - Each offering unique gameplay experiences
- **Mobile-Optimized** - Designed for 5-10 minute play sessions
- **Offline Play** - No internet required after initial download
- **No Pay-to-Win** - Focus on gameplay depth, not monetization
- **Free & Open Source** - Licensed under GPL-3.0

## ü§î Why This Project?

The football gaming community has long requested deep career mode experiences on mobile devices. While EA FC 26 offers comprehensive career modes on console (Manager Career, Player Career, and Create-a-Club), EA FC Mobile focuses primarily on card-collecting and quick matches, leaving a significant gap for players who want strategic, management-focused gameplay on mobile.

**This project aims to:**
- Provide the career mode depth that mobile football fans have been requesting
- Create an accessible, free alternative to expensive console gaming
- Demonstrate that complex simulation games can work on mobile platforms
- Build a community-driven football management experience
- Respect players' time and money (no pay-to-win mechanics)
